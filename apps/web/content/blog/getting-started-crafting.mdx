Crafting turns raw materials into tools, gear, and structures. The compendium makes it
easy to see exactly what each recipe needs and produces.

<Callout type="info">
Every recipe page lists its **inputs** and **outputs**, each linked to the item or cargo
involved — so you can trace a whole production chain by clicking through.
</Callout>

## Follow the craft graph

Open any item and look at **Made by** / **Used in**: those sections come straight from the
game's recipe data, so they're always in sync. Here's a common early-game material — click
through to see how it's made and where it's used:

<ItemCard slug="argent-ingot" />

From there, jump between inputs and outputs to plan everything you'll need to gather. Happy
crafting!
