BitCraft Companion is a fast, comprehensive companion for **BitCraft Online** — a
searchable compendium of items, cargo, buildings, and recipes, with the crafting
relationships that connect them.

<Callout>
The full compendium is live — browse Items, Cargo, Buildings, and Recipes from the
navigation above. Each entry has its own page with details and crafting links.
</Callout>

## What's here today

- **Compendium** — every item, cargo, building, and recipe, searchable and filterable.
- **Craft graph** — see what an item is *made by* and *used in*, linked both ways.
- **Guides** — how-tos and notes, like this one, that can embed live game data.

More to come. Thanks for stopping by!
